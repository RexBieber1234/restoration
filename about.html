About Restoration Christian Church

Welcome to Restoration Christian Church, a place of hope, healing, and transformation. Our mission is to spread the love of Christ to all nations, restore broken lives, and empower believers to live a victorious life through the Word of God.

Since our foundation, we have stood as a beacon of light to the community, offering support, guidance, and opportunities to grow in faith. Our church is more than a building — it’s a family where everyone is welcome.

We believe in powerful worship, deep teaching of the Scriptures, and community service that impacts lives. Join us for Sunday services, Bible studies, and outreach programs as we walk together in faith and unity.
